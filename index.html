Red Button

This is the Red Button app - an app for the elderly and mentally impaired.

Through a simple to use UI and utilisation of AI, the goal of the app is to make life easier for the user and their carer.

Three buttons allow the user to get help at their fingertips

Wether its a basic question, a healthcare need, or if they just need a chat, with three simple buttons

Red button can help!

(this MVP doesnt have any functionality, but shows off the general idea)
